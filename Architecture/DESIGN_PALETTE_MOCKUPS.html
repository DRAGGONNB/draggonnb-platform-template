Design Palette Recommendations

Which Palette is Right for DraggonnB CRMM?

1. Corporate Emerald - "The Trust Builder"

Best for: Conservative B2B audiences, established businesses, industries where trust is paramount

Psychological impact: Green = growth, money, stability, nature. Emerald specifically = premium + trustworthy

Competitive advantage: Differentiates from blue-heavy SaaS landscape

Recommendation: Choose this if target audience values reliability over innovation

2. Midnight Indigo - "The Premium Innovator"

Best for: Tech-forward audiences, innovation-focused businesses, premium positioning

Psychological impact: Indigo/purple = intelligence, wisdom, luxury, creativity, AI/tech

Competitive advantage: Aligns with AI-powered positioning, appeals to early adopters

Recommendation: Choose this if emphasizing cutting-edge AI and premium features

3. Obsidian Rose - "The Disruptor"

Best for: Bold brands, younger audiences, highly competitive markets needing differentiation

Psychological impact: Rose/red = energy, passion, action, urgency, confidence

Competitive advantage: Highly memorable, stands out in crowded B2B SaaS space

Recommendation: Choose this if targeting disruptive, fast-growth startups or creative industries

My Recommendation for DraggonnB CRMM:

Corporate Emerald with the option to A/B test against Midnight Indigo. Here's why: Your target audience (South African SMEs) values trust and proven ROI over bleeding-edge tech aesthetics. The emerald palette conveys growth (literally - your value prop is "scale your business") and financial success (green = money, profits). It differentiates you from the sea of blue SaaS products. However, Midnight Indigo works excellently if you want to emphasize the AI-powered intelligence aspect and position as premium/sophisticated. Obsidian Rose is bold but may be too aggressive for conservative B2B buyers making a R3,500/month commitment.
